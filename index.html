Overview

I was born in Seoul, South Korea and moved to San Diego when I was 2 years old. I have one older brother who is 3 years older than me, currently a second-year at USC. I attended Bright Horizon for preschool and Ocean Air Elementary with my favorite school year to this day being 3rd grade because I had a mix class of 2nd and 3rd graders making the class more fun. After 6th grade, I attended Pacific Trails Middle School where I met some lifelong friends which I am incredibly grateful for. I now attend Canyon Crest Academy where I am currently a junior, which is crazy to think about, and live a pretty average, routinely life.

Hobbies

In Kindergarten, I somehow discovered what gymnastics was and asked my mom to join the afterschool club. Once that became too easy and boring, I began going to TRC gymnastics, which is where I spent the next 10 years at. I trained in rec, then moved to Xcel, and finally to JO where I competed until level 7 and trained 8 for a few years, but continuously got injured which pulled me out of season twice. I decided to quit after missing 2 seasons in a row and turned to coaching, which is what I still do now. I do not have many other hobbies as I had put my full undivided attention into gymnastics, but I do love to eat and sleep, especially naps.
